I love doing impersonations of people.


I'm Benedict Cumberbatch, you may know me as Sherlock Holmes or as the sorcerer Dr. Strange in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Originally hailing from London, I was very closely attached to theater since my childhood as my parents, Timothy Carlton and Wanda Venthnam were well known Television actors.

Although in my childhood days, I was steered away from the stage when I was sent to Harrow Public School. Still it was theater that captured my imagination and I was awarded Arts Scholarship in my school.

I've portrayed titular characters like Stephen Hawking (2004), Sherlock (2010), Dr. Strange (2016) and many more in my entire career. Also I've been a part of many theatrical plays in the span of 15 - 20 years.

I always strive to give justice to the character that I portray on stage or on screen. I always commit to all my projects irrespective of its scale. Also, I am passionate about signing projects which are unique in its own-way.
